Chicken

Description

Chicken BBQ is a flavorful and smoky dish made by marinating chicken in a rich blend of spices, sauces, and seasonings before grilling it to perfection. The marinade typically includes ingredients like soy sauce, garlic, paprika, honey, and a hint of lemon juice to enhance the taste and tenderness of the meat. Some recipes also use a barbecue sauce glaze, which caramelizes beautifully over the heat, creating a crispy, slightly charred coating. Whether grilled over an open flame, baked, or cooked on a stovetop, BBQ chicken is a favorite for outdoor gatherings and family meals.

To prepare BBQ chicken, start by marinating the chicken for at least a few hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to penetrate deeply. Once marinated, grill the chicken over medium heat, turning occasionally and brushing it with extra barbecue sauce for a smoky and tangy finish. The key to juicy BBQ chicken is cooking it slowly to prevent drying out while ensuring a nice char on the outside. Serve the chicken hot with coleslaw, grilled vegetables, or corn on the cob for a delicious and satisfying meal.

    Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) barbecue sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  1. Steps
  2. In a large bowl, combine soy sauce, honey, olive oil, garlic, paprika, salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, and lemon juice. Mix well to combine.
  3. Add chicken thighs and drumsticks to the bowl, turning to coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
  4. Preheat grill to medium heat. Remove chicken from marinade and discard excess liquid.
  5. Grill chicken over indirect heat, turning occasionally, for about 30-40 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  6. Brush chicken with barbecue sauce during the last 10 minutes of cooking. Remove from grill and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
